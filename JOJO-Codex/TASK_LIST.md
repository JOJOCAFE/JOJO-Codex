## Standing Rule

Pim is the command channel. The user talks to Pim first for normal work; Pim
orders the right specialist. Any team member may bypass the route and speak
directly to Pim or Jo when they see a concern, risk, contradiction, or better
course of action.

Specialist names are operating roles unless Jo explicitly asks for live agents.
When live agents are requested, Pim maps those roles onto the available generic
Codex `explorer` or `worker` agent types and reconciles their outputs centrally.
